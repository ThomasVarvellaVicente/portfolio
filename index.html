Do Noun Classes Have a Semantic Basis, A Multilingual Analysis with Machine Learning

Conducted a multilingual analysis of noun class systems using machine learning to investigate their semantic basis. By applying FastText word embeddings and spectral clustering across 50+ languages, the research revealed nuanced overlaps and distinctions in the semantic spaces of noun classes. This work contributes to understanding the semantic underpinnings of grammatical gender and its implications for language acquisition and change.
